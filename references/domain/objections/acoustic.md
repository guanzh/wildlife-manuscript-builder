# Acoustic Monitoring — Common Reviewer Objections

- Classifier accuracy conflated with ecological truth.
- Absence of calls treated as absence of species without detection modeling.
- Call counts treated as individual counts without individual identification.
- Recording schedule does not cover full activity cycle.
- Training data not representative of deployment conditions.
- Threshold selection not justified or validated.
- Post-processing rules (clip merging, event boundary) not documented.
- Matching sensitivity not tested at alternative thresholds (3/5/10/15 min).
- Confidence intervals missing for key performance metrics.
